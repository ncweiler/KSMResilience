Living With Fire

Step into the role of a fire manager with the U.S. Forest Service and make difficult decisions to combat forest fires. Sometimes, in order to become more resilient to forest fires, you cannot put out every fire.

Hit the "click to play" button to start. Use the bar on the bottom to make your decisions when prompted.

The "explore topics" button on the lower right hand corner will let you explore different aspects of wildfire resilience as well as start the game over.
