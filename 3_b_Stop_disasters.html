Stop Disasters

  • Hit the "Launch Game" button followed by the "Play Game" button to start.
  • Choose from one of the five different disaster scenarios.
  • Pick a difficulty level. Easy is a good place to start!
  • To scroll through the map, move the cursor to the corner of the screen and click on the arrow that appears.
  • To construct new buildings or defenses, click on empty squares and chose either "Develop" or "Defense".
  • Don't forget to improve your existing buildings! Click on a building and select "Upgrades".
