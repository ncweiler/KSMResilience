Stop Disasters

  • Hit the "Launch Game" button followed by the "Play Game" button to start.
  • Choose from one of the five different disaster scenarios and choose a difficulty level. Easy is a good place to start.
  • Move the cursor to the corner of the screen and click on the arrow that appears to scroll through the map.
  • Click on empty squares to purchase buildings (by hitting the Develop button) and defenses (the defense button).
  • Don't forget to improve your existing buildings by clicking on them and then the Upgrades button.
