Stop Disasters

Do you have what it takes to protect these communities from an impending disaster? Invest your money in building improvements, infrastructure, mitigation measures and education in order to make the town more resilient. You can't buy everything so choose wisely.

Hit the "Launch Game" button followed by the "Play Game" button to start. Choose from one of the five different disaster scenarios and choose a difficulty level. Easy is a good place to start.

Click on empty squares to purchase buildings (by hitting the Develop button) and defenses (the defense button). Don't forget to improve your existing buildings by clicking on them and then the Upgrades button. Don't waste any time, the disaster is coming soon!
