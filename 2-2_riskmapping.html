Components of Risk

Hazard:
What could happen to trigger damage in a community?
Example of high hazard: Living on the gulf coast, hurricanes are a part of life.

Exposure:
What community assets are at stake in the event of a hazard?
Example of high exposure: Coastal buildings are particularly exposed to storm surges.

Vulnerability:
How sensitive are community assets to a hazard?
Example of high vulnerability: Aging infrastructure can fail catastrophically in a powerful storm.

Consequences:
The impact or damage caused by the hazard.
Example of high consequences: Hurricanes can bankrupt communities and destroy lives.
