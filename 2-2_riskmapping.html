Who's at Risk?

Components of Risk

Hazard: What could happen to trigger damage in a community?
For instance: Living on the gulf coast, hurricanes are a part of life.

Exposure: What community assets are at stake in the event of a hazard?
For instance: Coastal buildings are particularly exposed to storm surges.

Vulnerability: How sensitive are exposed community assets to the hazard?
For instance: Aging infrastructure can fail catastrophically in a powerful storm.

Consequences: The ultimate impact or damage caused by the hazard.
For instance: Hurricanes can bankrupt communities and destroy lives.
